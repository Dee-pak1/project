OUR STORY

WHO WE ARE

Introducing Nepal's Premier Tourism Service

Founded in 2010, our tourism agency has been committed to showcasing the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Nepal to travelers from around the world. What started as a small family business has grown into a leading tourism provider with a passion for authentic experiences.

Our team consists of experienced local guides who have intimate knowledge of Nepal's diverse landscapes, from the majestic Himalayas to the lush jungles of Chitwan. We pride ourselves on creating memorable journeys that connect travelers with the heart and soul of Nepal.

OUR MISSION

WHAT WE BELIEVE IN

Sustainable Tourism

We are committed to environmentally conscious travel practices that preserve Nepal's natural beauty for generations to come. All our tours follow eco-friendly guidelines and support local conservation efforts.

Community Empowerment

We believe in giving back to the communities we visit. A portion of all tour proceeds goes directly to local education and infrastructure projects throughout Nepal's rural regions.

Authentic Experiences

We strive to provide genuine cultural immersion beyond typical tourist attractions. Our tours include homestays, traditional cooking classes, and meetings with local artisans and spiritual leaders.
